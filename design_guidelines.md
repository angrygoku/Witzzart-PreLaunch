# Witzzart Pre-Launch Landing Page Design Guidelines

## Design Approach
**Reference-Based Approach**: Inspired by Behance and Creative Market, focusing on vibrant creative community aesthetics with professional marketplace credibility. The design emphasizes artistic flair while maintaining government-backed trustworthiness.

## Core Design Elements

### A. Color Palette
**Primary Colors:**
- Brand Magenta: 331 83% 56% (#E91E63) - Main CTA buttons, key highlights
- Creative Purple: 262 48% 48% (#673AB7) - Secondary elements, creative sections
- Warm Orange: 36 100% 50% (#FF9800) - Accent highlights, creative touches

**Supporting Colors:**
- Background: 0 0% 98% (#FAFAFA) - Main background
- Text Primary: 0 0% 13% (#212121) - Headings and body text
- Trust Green: 122 39% 49% (#4CAF50) - Government badges and trust indicators

### B. Typography
- **Primary**: Poppins (headings, CTAs) - Modern, friendly, creative
- **Secondary**: Montserrat (body text, descriptions) - Clean, professional readability
- **Hierarchy**: Bold headings (24-48px), medium subheadings (18-24px), regular body (16px)

### C. Layout System
**Spacing**: Tailwind units of 4, 6, 8, 12, 16 for consistent rhythm
- Generous whitespace between sections (16-24 units)
- Component padding: 6-8 units
- Element margins: 4-6 units

### D. Component Library
**Navigation**: Floating header with brand logo and key links
**Hero**: Large impactful section with "Launching Soon" badge and mission statement
**Feature Cards**: Asymmetrical grid showcasing platform capabilities with icons
**Government Section**: Dedicated trust indicator area with official badges
**Contact Forms**: Clean, accessible forms with clear CTAs
**Buttons**: Primary (magenta), Secondary (purple outline), WhatsApp (green)

## Visual Treatment & Gradients
**Creative Gradients**: 
- Hero background: Subtle magenta to purple diagonal (331 83% 56% to 262 48% 48%)
- Feature section overlays: Light orange to transparent for visual depth
- Government section: Trust green to light green for credibility

**Background Treatments**: 
- Floating geometric elements in brand colors with low opacity
- Asymmetrical section layouts with creative positioning
- Subtle texture overlays maintaining readability

## Key Sections (Maximum 4)
1. **Hero Section**: "Launching Soon" with mission statement and WhatsApp CTA
2. **Platform Capabilities**: Creative products, events, job hirings with visual icons
3. **Government Trust**: Rajasthan State and Central Government backing indicators
4. **Contact Section**: Artist/venue/organizer contact form with email integration

## Images
**Large Hero Image**: Yes - Creative collage featuring diverse artists, venues, and cultural elements from Rajasthan, positioned as background with gradient overlay
**Feature Icons**: Custom illustrated icons representing marketplace capabilities (paintbrush, event tickets, job listings)
**Government Logos**: Official Rajasthan State and Central Government emblems in dedicated trust section
**Background Elements**: Floating creative assets (paint splashes, musical notes, art tools) as decorative elements

## Mobile Responsiveness
Stack asymmetrical layouts vertically, maintain generous spacing, ensure touch-friendly button sizes (minimum 44px), optimize hero image for mobile viewports.
